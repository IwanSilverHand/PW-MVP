Atoms & Molecules

Atoms are the building blocks on which the page is built. They include basic HTML elements such as tags, form fields, buttons, images, and other elements that cannot be broken down into smaller elements without losing their functionality.

The atoms that we can find in almost every web project have been added here. You can use them as a base to create your own and add or remove atoms depending on the needs of your project.
‍
And Molecules are relatively simple groups of UI elements functioning as a unit, that have atoms inside.
‍
As the molecules are totally dependent on the design, they will be unique for each project. On this page we show you two examples to illustrate this typology of elements, but it is up to you to create the right molecules according to your design needs. Use atoms to create the molecules that will define the interface of your website.
